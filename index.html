🔗 Connect OneDrive

Do this once and all teachers can upload and view files instantly — no extra apps needed.

  1. Go to OneDrive (onedrive.live.com or your school's Microsoft 365) and create a folder named TNHS Lesson Plans.

  2. Right-click the folder → Share → Change access to "Anyone with the link" → Copy the link.

  3. Paste the folder link below and click Save.

💡 The link looks like: https://onedrive.live.com/... or https://tupd-my.sharepoint.com/...
This is saved in the browser. Each colleague only needs to paste it once, or you can share the already-configured link.
